# Contribution Policy

## Position

This is a **doctrinal work**, not a collaborative framework.

External input is welcome.
Authority is not delegated.

---

## Accepted Contributions

The following may be proposed:
- clarifications
- errors or inconsistencies
- references or lineage material
- empirical counter-examples

---

## Not Accepted

The following will not be incorporated:
- alternative doctrines
- stylistic rewrites
- consulting methodologies
- framework extensions branded by third parties

---

## Process

Contributions may be submitted as:
- issues
- pull requests
- private correspondence

All contributions are reviewed by the author.
