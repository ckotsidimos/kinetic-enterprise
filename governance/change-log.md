# Change Log

## Purpose

This log records **what changed and why**, not activity for its own sake.

Only changes that affect interpretation, structure, or citation are recorded.

---

## Format

Each entry must include:
- version
- date
- type of change
- rationale

---

## Example

### v2.1 — 2025-12-17
**Type:** MINOR  
**Change:** Introduced Capability Maneuver Loop  
**Rationale:** Required to explain continuous adaptation without transformation programs

---

### v2.0 — 2025-11-30
**Type:** MAJOR  
**Change:** Formalized Kinetic Enterprise as a doctrine  
**Rationale:** Prior material implied a framework; doctrine status made explicit
